Our Approach to Corporate Responsibility

Our corporate responsibility framework leverages our distinct scientific capabilities to tackle the most pressing challenges facing our communities and our operations. Driven by our core ethos of "Doing Well by Doing Good," our strategic vision is anchored in three primary pillars: transforming patient outcomes, cultivating an environment of unyielding integrity, and establishing highly resilient, sustainable communities.

Our strategic responsibility milestones

Our 2025 responsibility objectives are deeply aligned with our core mandate: delivering groundbreaking therapeutics to patients confronting severe health crises. Through extensive collaboration with key stakeholders and alignment with premier global standards—most notably the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)—we have formalized 12 comprehensive responsibility targets alongside robust environmental commitments.

1. Enhance outcomes for patients facing critical illnesses ▼

We are committed to developing and delivering transformative medicines that address serious health challenges. Our patient-centric approach ensures that every research decision prioritizes improving quality of life and health outcomes for those we serve.

Through innovative clinical trial designs, patient advocacy partnerships, and expanded access programs, we work to ensure that life-saving therapies reach those who need them most.

2. Cultivate a culture defined by excellence and ethical integrity ▼

Our commitment to operational excellence and ethical conduct forms the foundation of everything we do. We maintain the highest standards of compliance, transparency, and accountability across all our operations.

Through comprehensive training programs, robust governance structures, and a speak-up culture, we ensure that integrity remains at the heart of our scientific innovation.

3. Engineer a sustainable future for our communities ▼

We are dedicated to building resilient, sustainable communities through environmental stewardship, social investment, and economic contribution. Our sustainability initiatives span carbon reduction, waste minimization, and community engagement.

By aligning our business strategy with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, we create lasting positive impact for patients, employees, and society at large.

Operational and board-level oversight

The Corporate Governance and Compliance Committee of our Board of Directors provides rigorous oversight of our ESG initiatives, conducting comprehensive strategic reviews at least annually. Operationally, our cross-disciplinary Responsibility Committee—comprised of senior leadership—drives the day-to-day execution of our strategy, ensuring strict adherence to our established metrics and milestones.

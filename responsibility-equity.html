Health Equity & Access to Medicine

Ensuring medicines reach patients who need them most

Global Access to Medicines

We believe life-saving treatments should be accessible to everyone, regardless of geography or income. We employ tiered pricing strategies, voluntary licensing, and product donations to expand access in low- and middle-income countries.

Diversity in Clinical Trials

Ensuring our medicines are safe and effective for all populations requires diverse representation in clinical research. We actively partner with community organizations to increase trial participation among historically underrepresented demographics.

Advancing Health Equity

Strengthening Health Systems

Investing in healthcare infrastructure and workforce training in underserved regions to build long-term capacity for patient care.

Patient Assistance Programs

Providing financial support and co-pay assistance to ensure eligible patients in developed markets can afford their prescribed therapies.

Addressing Disparities

Funding independent research and community programs aimed at identifying and dismantling systemic barriers to quality healthcare.

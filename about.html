About CellveraPharma

20 years of advancing medicine and improving lives worldwide

Our Mission

Founded in 2003, CellveraPharma is a global biopharmaceutical company dedicated to discovering, developing, and delivering innovative medicines that address serious health challenges.

Our team of over 5,000 scientists, researchers, and healthcare professionals work across 15 research facilities worldwide, united by a shared mission to improve patient outcomes.

We invest significantly in research and development, with over $800 million invested annually in discovering the next generation of treatments.

Our Values

  • Patient First: Every decision starts with the patient
  • Scientific Excellence: Pushing boundaries of what's possible
  • Integrity: Transparent and ethical in all we do
  • Collaboration: Working together across disciplines
  • Innovation: Embracing new approaches and technologies
